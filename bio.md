# Thong Pham — Professional Bio

Thong Pham is a growth marketing executive based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, with 15+ years building and scaling digital businesses across Southeast Asia. He holds a Bachelor of Business Administration from the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City.

His expertise spans growth strategy, digital marketing, e-commerce, and fintech, with deep experience in customer acquisition, retention, CRM systems, and cross-functional go-to-market execution.

As Marketing Director at ZaloPay (VNG Group), Thong built the marketing team from scratch and drove 10x Monthly Active User growth, establishing ZaloPay as Vietnam's 2nd largest e-wallet. As CMO at Lazada Vietnam (2015–2018), he led 30+ marketing professionals through triple-digit year-on-year growth ahead of Alibaba's acquisition in 2016. He also founded Ahometo.com (now Mitssy.com) — Vietnam's first home décor e-commerce startup — securing backing from 5Desire incubator and angel investors. Earlier, he grew 2 of 3 managed Fabric Care brands into P&G's top 5 fastest-growing regionally, and earned a Service Excellence Award at Nielsen.

Thong is seeking advanced academic training in business strategy and data analytics to deepen his impact on Southeast Asia's rapidly evolving digital economy.
