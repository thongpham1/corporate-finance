## 2. Selection Rationale

PNJ is selected for three reasons:

**Industry relevance.** As a consumer-facing retail and manufacturing business, PNJ's financial structure — gross margins, inventory turnover, working capital cycles — maps directly to concepts covered in BUS-629. The company's dual role as manufacturer and retailer makes its ratio analysis richer than a single-segment business.

**Analytical interest.** Gold price volatility and shifting consumer spending patterns in Vietnam create meaningful year-on-year variation in PNJ's financial ratios. This makes it a compelling case for hypothesis-driven analysis: understanding *why* ratios move, not just reporting that they moved.

**Career relevance.** With background in e-commerce (Lazada) and fintech (ZaloPay), I am interested in how Vietnam's leading omnichannel consumer brands manage the transition from physical retail to digital commerce. PNJ's growing online channel and loyalty program (PNJ Silver) make it directly relevant to my professional context.

## 4. Preliminary Observations & Hypotheses

1. **Gross margin compression in gold-heavy years:** When gold prices spike (as in 2024), revenue increases but gross margin may compress as raw material costs rise faster than retail pricing adjustments. *Hypothesis: Gross margin declined in FY2024 vs. FY2023.*

2. **Inventory turnover slowdown post-COVID recovery:** PNJ expanded aggressively in 2022–2023. A rapid store rollout typically pressures inventory turnover as new locations ramp up. *Hypothesis: Inventory turnover ratio trended lower in 2023 vs. 2022.*

3. **ROE remains above 20% driven by asset efficiency:** PNJ's vertically integrated model and high brand premium historically sustain strong ROE. *Hypothesis: ROE remained above 20% in FY2023, supported by asset turnover rather than leverage.*

## 5. Ratio Categories Preview

Given PNJ's retail + manufacturing model, the most relevant ratio categories are:

| Category | Rationale |
|---|---|
| **Profitability** (Gross margin, Net margin, ROE, ROA) | Core measure of pricing power and capital efficiency |
| **Efficiency** (Inventory turnover, Asset turnover) | Critical for a multi-SKU jewelry retailer with 700+ stores |
| **Liquidity** (Current ratio, Quick ratio) | Working capital management under gold price swings |
| **Leverage** (D/E, Interest coverage) | PNJ carries moderate debt; coverage ratio tracks debt sustainability |
| **Du Pont Decomposition** | Separates margin, turnover, and leverage contributions to ROE |
